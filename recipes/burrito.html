Crack 'A' Lackin camp-out burrito

The tale of the great camp out burrito

Looking for that convenient, filling and fun food to entertain everyone,
from the kids that just have unlimited energy to your ole pa who insists only he can navigate you all safely?
I introduce to you, the humble burrito! Enjoy!

Good to know details!

  • Preparation time: 5 minuets
  • Cooking time: 20 minuets
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1lb of browned bacon
  • 8 eggs - scrambled
  • Green onions - sliced
  • Cheddar cheese - grated
  • Any other additional toppings/ sauces etc

Right! Let's whip this burrito up!

  1. Mix all cooked ingredients together.
  2. Fold in a tortilla. Wrap in foil.
  3. Add to plastic zipper bag or portable container. Until ready to eat.
  4. Take out when ready to eat.
  5. Cook 10-15 min or until cheese is melted.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
